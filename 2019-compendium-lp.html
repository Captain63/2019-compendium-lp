Innovation in Government® Report
2019 Year in Review

2019 was a year of monumental shifts across the public sector IT landscape, from high-profile acquisitions among solutions providers to accelerating adoption of key modernization legislation such as FedRAMP and FITARA. To help government leaders keep their focus on mission goals, Carahsoft has partnered with FCW to showcase the latest innovations from its ecosystem of technology partners in its Innovation in Government® report.

To aid with 2020 planning, we have compiled all 7 of these reports from 2019 into one comprehensive compendium. Download this compendium to learn the latest developments across multiple technology verticals, including Cloud Adoption, Cybersecurity, Customer Experience and Artificial Intelligence.

Featuring insights and use cases from 40 technology leaders and 9 government executives, including Federal CIO Suzette Kent.
